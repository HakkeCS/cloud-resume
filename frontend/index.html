About Me

My name is Jake! I currently work as a database developer/administrator for a public high school district. I am a veteran of the US Navy Nuclear Power Program and a former shipbuilder. I got started in the tech industry in 2020 as a level 1 desktop support agent and fell in love with the work and the community. Within one year, I moved up to a level 2 support role where I was exposed to more systems level technologies. I worked extensively with active directory and deployment managers as well as the google admin console.

In November of 2021 I was offered my current position with little SQL experience and I was thrust into the world of database management and development and had to learn quickly to adapt. I loved what I learned and my experiences set me down the path towards learning everything I can about database and server management. My goal is to continue learning about IT and cloud computing and work towards cloud based certifications including DevOps Engineer and Cloud Architect.
